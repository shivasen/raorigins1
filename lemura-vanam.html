Lemura Vanam

Ancient Tamil healing systems and herbal medicine revival.

The Essence of Lemura Vanam

Lemura Vanam is committed to the research, preservation, and practice of ancient Tamil medical systems. This stage aims to bring back the profound knowledge of Siddha medicine, which views health as a holistic balance of mind, body, and spirit. By recovering lost knowledge, we can offer natural and effective healing modalities to the modern world.

Core Objectives

  • Three Medical Systems: Researching and applying the principles of Neetru (Hydropathy), Ootru (Spring water therapy), and Vaattru (Distillation therapy).
  • Ancient Medical Manuscripts Recovery: Actively seeking and translating 87 ancient medical manuscripts to unlock their secrets.
  • Ravana's Rasam Medical System Revival: Focusing on the specialized revival of the Rasam (Alchemy) medical system attributed to the sage-king Ravana.
